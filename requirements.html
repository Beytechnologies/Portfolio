Travel Requirements

What You Need to Travel for Hajj or Umrah

  • Valid international passport with at least 6 months validity
  • Recent passport photographs (white background)
  • National Identification Number (NIN)
  • Vaccination card (e.g., Yellow fever, Meningitis, COVID-19 if applicable)
  • Proof of payment for package
  • Completed registration form
  • Any additional Saudi Arabian government requirements (we will inform you)
